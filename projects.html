Projects done so far

1. A Geo-Intelligent Crop Advisor and Market Predictor System for Farmers:-

Developing the machine learning and deep learning model to recommend crops based on the analysis of soil images, considering key soil parameters such as pH, rainfall, nutrient levels (nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium), along with geographical regions.

2. Heart Beat Sensor:-

"Heart Beat Sensor Using PIC16F877A," is designed to provide a practical solution for real-time heart rate monitoring. By utilising the capabilities of the PIC16F877A microcontroller and a heart rate sensor, we aim to accurately capture and process heart rate signals. The project aims to empower individuals to monitor their heart rate conveniently, promoting personal health awareness and well-being.
